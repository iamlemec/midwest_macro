The University of Pittsburgh is hosting the Fall 2017 Midwest Macroeconomics Meetings on November 3-5, 2017.

Xavier Gabaix (Harvard) and Dirk Krueger (Penn) will be the plenary speakers.

The Program Committee is:

  • Marla Ripoll (Pitt)
  • Daniele Coen-Pirani (Pitt)
  • Stefania Albanesi (Pitt)
  • Douglas Hanley (Pitt)
  • Sewon Hur (Pitt)
  • Shu Lin Wee (CMU)
  • David Childers (CMU)

Submissions will be open from July 1 to August 10, 2017 through Conference Maker.

Decisions will be announced by September 1, 2017
