The University of Pittsburgh is hosting the Fall 2017 Midwest Macroeconomics Meetings on November 3-5, 2017. Xavier Gabaix (Harvard) and Dirk Krueger (Penn) will be the plenary speakers.

The Program Committee members are:

  • Marla Ripoll (Co-chair, Pitt)
  • Daniele Coen-Pirani (Co-chair, Pitt)
  • Stefania Albanesi (Pitt)
  • Douglas Hanley (Pitt)
  • Sewon Hur (Pitt)
  • David Childers (CMU)
  • Shu Lin Wee (CMU)

The conference venue is David Lawrence Hall (map).
